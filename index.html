Why MediHacks?

A Fun Hackathon Experience

Whether this is your first hackathon ever or you can do one in your sleep, MediHacks is a great way to show off your hacking skills and apply your knowledge to real-world solutions.

Experienced Judges and Speakers

With judges and guest speakers from students like you to empoloyees from tech companies across the world, you can get incredible feedback on your projects at MediHacks.

All-Inclusive

At MediHacks, we are striving to be an inclusive event for all ages, skill levels, and backgrounds.

Meet the Organizers

Harjyot Sahni

Harjyot Sahni is a rising junior at Saratoga High School who has competed in numerous hackathons. This is his second time hosting a hackathon and he is excited to create a great hackathon experience for students around the world.

Dhanush Ekollu

Dhanush is a rising junior at Saratoga High School who has competed in numerous hackathons. He is working on bringing in sponsors from various tech companies to bring in guest speakers and sponsors to make this hackathon great for participants.

Tanuj Siripurapu

Tanuj Siripurapu is a rising junior at Saratoga High School who has competed in and won numerous other hackathons. Having much experience, he is very excited to try and make this the best possible experience for first-timers as well as more advanced hackers.
