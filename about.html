About Us

Our Mission

At MediHacks, we strive to host inclusive events for all ages, skill levels, and backgrounds. Whether this is your first hackathon ever or you can do one in your sleep, MediHacks is a great way to show off your hacking skills and apply your knowledge to real-world solutions.

With judges and guest speakers from students like you to empoloyees from tech companies across the world, you can get incredible feedback on your projects at MediHacks, to better develop the tools to make tommorow a healthier place for all.
